- WFH Monday
  An idea local uses (or has considered, I'm not sure which) is to have
  everybody work from home on one day holding meetings, conversations, etc
  online in order to facilitate understanding of remote devs, groupt inclusion,
  etc. If we all worked from home on a monday, when meetings and such are the
  norm, it would fill this need

- No Meeting... Wednesdays?
  Why not have no meeting Wednesdays or some other day during the week? The
  purpose of the no meeting workday is to get stuff done. however, Fridays lend
  themself to lazy days so we could maximize the no meeting day by:
  - agreeing everybody be in the office for a given day
  - on that day, we do not have any meetings (perhaps outside of standup)

  This would allow tons of time for focused dev while physically in the same
  space as those we rely on. It lends itself well towards team building as well
  as simple things like pairing, ad-hoc communication, etc
