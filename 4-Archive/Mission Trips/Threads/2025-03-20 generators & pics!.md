Hi team,

I just got off the phone with Cesar Gonzalez, one of the leaders on base. He is preparing for our team and it's getting real!  We will have some opportunities to be flexible!

We will be joining a few other teams from around the USA on base, with 141 people total.

They still do not have city electricity, and water is brought in with tanks. They are operating on generators. We have to be very careful not to waste water. There are only 10 showers available so we will be bathing according to a schedule that Cesar gives us 😳

Not sure if we will be in the army tents, sleep trailers or bunks yet. Because they are running on generators, they do not have fantastic heat. If it gets cold at night, we will be cold. He said he had 3 blankets the other night and was still cold. So pack warm blankets and maybe even a coat to sleep in (Cesar's suggestion).

He says it is rocky and not well lit.  Bring flashlights or headlamps.  Headlamps are good if you want to see inside the port a potty at night.

If you need a CPAP machine for sleeping tell him asap upon arrival so he knows you need a generator. They have others with this need and it's not a problem.

Dress modestly- no super tight stuff or spaghetti straps.

I'm attaching a few pictures he sent. 😊
