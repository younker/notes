Hello Jason,


We are very excited to welcome you to the Harris team on Monday, March 31st!  Below are a few updates we'd like to share with you prior to your first day.


I9 Documentation

In order to complete your I9 verification, we will need to see one document from the USCIS List A (which verifies your identity and authorization to work). Or, we will need to see two documents: one from List B (validates your identity) and one from List C (shows that you’re authorized to work in the US). The lists can be found here. i-9 acceptable documents<https://www.uscis.gov/i-9-central/form-i-9-acceptable-documents>

Please review the requirements and send me photos of your documents on your first day.


Laptop Delivery

The IT team will send out a laptop for you via FedEx.  A signature is required upon delivery. They will share the tracking number with you on a separate email.


Your schedule for Monday, March 31st is listed below:


9:30 – 10am EST: IT Orientation

This meeting will help you log into Harris systems for the first time. You've been invited to attend to your personal email address so you can access the call easily before you can log into your work email account. You won’t be able to log into your laptop until this call.


12-1 pm EST: HR and Benefits Orientation

This meeting will be an overview of HR policies, benefits, payroll, and all of the things the HR team manages to make sure your experience at Harris is a consistently positive one.
