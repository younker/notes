 INTERVIEW TIPS

  1.  What are you wearing (collared shirt, button down shirt, etc.)?  "Don't dress down on your way up!"
  2.  Avoid, "YES" or "NO" answers.  Provide examples of projects or experience when asked about a certain technology.
  3.  Know the company you are meeting with!  Review their website for their industry, products, size, milestones, etc and write down intelligent questions based of this info to show that you are interested in what they do.
  4.  As the interview is winding up, ask any questions you have about the roles and responsibilities of the job that have not been clarified.
  5.  Once that is done, ask if there are any questions or concerns that they have about your background so you have the opportunity to address them before you leave.
  6.  Express interest in the job before wrapping up!  Show your desire and confidence and ask for that next step!
  7.  DO NOT talk about money, we'll handle that for you.
  8.  If you do not want the job, DO NOT decline right there on the spot. Please allow Jobot to deliver that news.

What does Bera.ai Do?
Bera.ai is an AI-driven company that focuses on brand intelligence and consumer insights. They help companies measure and improve the emotional connection between brands and their customers, providing tools to assess brand loyalty and growth opportunities.
Key aspects of Bera.ai’s offerings:

  1.  Brand Relationship Measurement: Bera.ai provides detailed insights into how consumers feel about a brand, analyzing emotional engagement, brand perceptions, and loyalty.
  2.  Predictive Analytics: They use AI-powered predictive models to forecast future customer behavior, identifying which brand attributes and consumer groups drive long-term loyalty and growth.
  3.  Consumer Insights: Bera.ai helps businesses understand their target audience by offering deep insights into consumer preferences, behaviors, and motivations, which can then be used to shape marketing strategies and campaigns.
  4.  Data Visualization Tools: Their platform provides easy-to-read dashboards and reports that help brands monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize brand strategies.
  5.  Strategic Consulting: In addition to providing data, Bera.ai also offers strategic consulting to help companies implement the insights and drive better marketing and brand positioning decisions.

They focus on helping companies build stronger emotional connections with their customers, ultimately driving brand growth and value.
