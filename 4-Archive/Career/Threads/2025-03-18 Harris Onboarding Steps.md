## 1. 2025-03-18 15:31 — kat.grolle@harrispoll.com

Hi Jason,

Welcome to Harris! We are so excited to welcome you aboard on March 31st!  I will be happy to guide you through our onboarding steps and will be able to assist you with any questions you may have from this point on. To start the onboarding process, here are our next steps:


  1.  You will receive an email from Sterling, our background check provider. Please make an effort to complete the background check request promptly as it can take some time for verification of all information to be completed.


  1.  You will receive an email from UKG, our onboarding platform, to walk you through the necessary employment documents and info such as tax forms, direct deposit information, etc. Please have this completed by  Wednesday, March 19th. We will ask you to verify your I9 documentation on your first day.


  1.  Your laptop will be shipped to the address you provide using this MS Form here IT Request Form<https://forms.office.com/r/nkXCkFHHv7>. Please also choose which extra IT supplies you may need as soon as possible.  Our Stagwell tech team will reach out prior to your first day to confirm delivery date of laptop and tracking number. They will be adding an IT Onboarding call to your calendar on the morning of March 31st. This will be sent to your personal email as you will not have access to your work email before this call.


  1.  Bin and I will reach out to you the week before your start date to get you prepped for your first day.


  1.  As part of our onboarding process, we like to share a new hire announcement with staff to allow staff to connect with our new hires more naturally. To help us would you share a few sentences briefly describing your work history prior to Harris and any hobbies/interests you may have as well as headshot/photo you would be comfortable sharing? We will ask to have this by your first day.


In the meantime, feel free to reach out if you have any questions. Welcome to the team! 😊

Best,
Kat
