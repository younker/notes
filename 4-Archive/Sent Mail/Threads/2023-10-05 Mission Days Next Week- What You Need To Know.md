> Dear HLCS Parents,
>
> It's our favorite time of the year, because we have our fall mission days right around the corner! Next week, October 12-13, we will be sending our students out into the community to love and serve our neighbors! We are writing to (1) inform you on what those days will look like for your children and what they need to bring along with them, (2) ask for volunteer chaperones, and (3) create congruence between staff and parents about the purpose of mission days.
>
>
> 1) What You Need To Know:
> Mission days' assignments/teams will be given on Wednesday, October 11th.
> Next week Thursday and Friday (Oct. 12-13) we will meet in the sanctuary for a time of worship and devotions at 8 a.m., and then we will launch our teams into their activities.
>
> What Your Student Should Bring:
> All students need to bring a packed lunch. They should also bring a water bottle and should dress in layers.
> It may benefit them to bring work gloves, boots, and extra layers of warm clothing - especially if their assignment is outside.
> We are in need of rakes! If you have any available rakes, please send them with your child.
>
> 2) We are in need of parents that are willing to chaperone and work alongside our students.
> If you are interested in helping us (even for a partial day), that would be immensely helpful.
>
> 3) The Heart Behind Mission Days
> We are fully aware of the fact that mission days can be uncomfortable for your children. They may be in a group without their friends, may have to do work in cold or wet weather conditions, or may be asked to do work that's outside of their comfort zone! That being said, we ask that you partner with us in encouraging students to serve in the midst of their discomfort. The purpose of mission days is to meet needs in our community and create a heart posture of being others' focused within our students. Please pray for your children to grow and be challenged by their experiences.
>
> We are praying that the students are reminded what a blessing it is to bless others.
>
> With love and excitement,
> Heather Standish and Kiley Graham
