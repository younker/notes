> Belle Tire 13U A
>
> Games watched: 1/9 vs Plymouth Flyers (White) 13U A and 1/11 vs Kensington Valley 13U A
>
> Players to watch:
>
> #17 – Left-shooting defensemen, is their top player, smaller but plays very physical when he recognizes he will not be able to take the puck.  He will play deep in the offensive zone and pinches a lot.
>
> #23 – Right-shooting defensemen, bigger and more physical.  Is never paired with #17 and drives plays into the offensive zone as well.
>
> A few more points:
>
> -          The players communicate and cover the net front very well.
>
> -          Coached to make the hit along the boards if the puck is in the corner rather than poke at the puck.  They will hold players into the boards.
>
> -          Their goalie is pretty good.  They were outplayed by Kensington Valley but their tight defense and very good goaltending made the difference as they still won 3-2.
>
> -          Their goalies will play the puck and are instructed to aim for the boards
>
> -          Their other players are physical and don’t seem to be bothered by taking a lot of penalties
