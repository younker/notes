Belle Tire 13U A

Games watched: 1/9 vs Plymouth Flyers (White) 13U A and 1/11vs Kensington Valley 13U A

Players to watch:

#17 – Left-shooting defensemen, is their top player, smallerbut plays very physical when he recognizes he will not be able to take the puck.  He will play deep in the offensive zone andpinches a lot.

#23 – Right-shooting defensemen, bigger and more physical.  Is never paired with #17 and drives playsinto the offensive zone as well.

A few more points:

-         The players communicate and cover the net frontvery well.

-         Coached to make the hit along the boards if thepuck is in the corner rather than poke at the puck.  They will hold players into the boards.

-         Their goalie is pretty good.  They were outplayed by Kensington Valley buttheir tight defense and very good goaltending made the difference as they still won 3-2.

-         Their goalies will play the puck and areinstructed to aim for the boards

-         Their other players are physical and don’t seemto be bothered by taking a lot of penalties
