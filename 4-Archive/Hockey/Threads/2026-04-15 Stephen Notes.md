## 1. 2026-04-15 09:05 — 2012hhcsilver@gmail.com

Good Morning,

Can I just say how much of a pleasure your son is to have.  After every practice and game thus far he has been grateful and anxious to learn what to work on.  Extremely refreshing.  He has a ton of raw talent, just needs a little refinement.

Last night I gave him two things.  I am sending you the two things so that you can reinforce if he’s doing any additional garage work or skating in between games.

1. He is not utilizing his size and strength to win over pucks.  He is often trying to use his stick to swipe pucks away, when he is strong enough and fast enough to just lift their stick and strip the puck away.  The video link below was the closest thing I could find to what I showed him after the game, but it shows it in a sequence of stick, then body, then puck.  I think stick and body should be simultaneous, or body should be first.  I told him we don’t need big hits, that the key is getting our shoulder in front of the puck carriers, while lifting the stick.  He is big and strong enough that he should be able to take the puck at will against most teams and players we face.  He just needs that technique.

2. He’s waiting too long to shoot.  Between him waiting for a more perfect shot and/or a defender catching up, he isn’t getting shots off.  Yesterday he probably had 4-5 instances that could have been shots on net that didn’t even result in a shot attempt.  I expressed that to start, we’re not looking to pick corners, we’re looking to get it on net as quickly and as hard as possible.  I’ve seen him rifle shots that would cause havoc for goalies even if it hits them squarely in the chest.  Yesterday was the first time that the new players were exposed to our philosophy on "garbage goalies.”  Simply meaning that we prefer the scrappy rebounds and deflections over the pretty goals, because pretty opportunities don’t come around often enough.

Let me know if you have any questions.  It feels like it is still an adjustment, but I truly feel Stephen is our ace in the hole.  Just needs the minor tweaks.

P.S. I am missing practice Saturday and it seems like a large portion of our team is also missing.  You’d be justified to skip the drive if you wish.  Of course, feel free to still attend, just trying to be conscientious with your time and money.
