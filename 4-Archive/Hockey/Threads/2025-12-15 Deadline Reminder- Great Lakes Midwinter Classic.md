Hello! With the *Great Lakes Midwinter Classic* just one month away, we are
beginning to finalize the hotel assignments and noticed that you have not
started the hotel process. We are needing your team's rooming list and
hotel preferences entered and finalized in our system no later than *Tuesday,
December 16th, 2025 at 5:00 PM (CST).*

You should have received a previous email from our company with login
information for our Event Management System. If you do not have this
information, use the following link to request a password reset email for
our system. Your login ID is your email address that you are receiving this
email at.

PASSWORD RESET <https://www.myhockeytournaments.com/password/reset>

For your convenience, I have outlined  the process for selecting your
hotels and entering your team's rooming list below.

   - Once you are in the system, you may need to update your profile.
   - Once you have updated your profile, you will be taken to the main page
   where you will see Rooming List, Hotel Preferences & Roster.
   - You will begin the process by clicking on Required under Rooming List.
   You must provide a parent name & email address for each family and coach
   that will need a room for the tournament. Also, if you have a family that
   is divorced, you can list both parents and they will let me know if they
   will not be attending. You have the option to add additional information if
   you would like but it is not required to finalize the rooming list
   information. The additional information is arrival/departure dates,
   occupancy and a credit card #. Once you have entered and saved the rooming
   list, an auto-generated email will be sent to each family to finish
   entering their rooming information.
   - Once all families have completed their required rooming information,
   you will be notified that you can log back into the system to select your
   hotel preferences.
   - If your team is traveling with another team(s), there is a Special
   Requests button on the Hotel Preference tab to make a note to keep the
   teams together. It is helpful for all teams that want to be placed together
   to complete this information so that it is noted in the system.

The hotel will receive the rooming list you provide and will hold rooms
with the credit card(s) provided. Nothing is charged to the credit cards
provided until arrival at the hotel but please note that in the event a
family does not show up and/or does not cancel prior to the 72 hour (3
days) deadline the credit card provided in our system will be charged by
the hotel for a 1 night stay. Per the hotel terms & conditions, information
on your team's hotel, hotel contact person and game schedule will be
available at least 14 days prior to the start of the tournament.

Thank you for your attention to this matter and please do not hesitate to
reach out to me if you have any questions or need any assistance.
