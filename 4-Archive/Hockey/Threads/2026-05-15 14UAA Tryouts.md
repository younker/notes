## 2. 2026-05-17 19:07 — wheeler6415@gmail.com

All,


Again, welcome to the 14UAA tryouts for the 2026/2027 season. Below are a
few important details as we move into the next couple of days:


*1) **Check-In*
Check-in will open at 6:45 PM. I will be set up at the back of the lobby.
All players must check in with me.

If, for some reason, your player arrives before I do, please have them
place their bags down and then come check in with me as soon as I arrive.


*2)** Ice Times*
We are on the ice Monday and Tuesday at Centre Ice Arena (Molon
Rink) from 7:30–8:20 PM.


*3) Groups / Jersey Colors*
We will be running three groups. Group assignments and jersey colors are
listed below.

If your player already has the assigned jersey color, please wear it. For
example, for our TC players, please wear your Red or White jersey with your
last name on it.

It does not matter if multiple players have the same number. From an
evaluation standpoint, it is much easier for coaches to identify players by
last name than by small pin-on numbers.

I will also have extra Red, White, and Black jerseys available if your
player doesn't have that color. That is completely fine.

Again, if your player already has the assigned jersey color with their last
name on it, please wear that jersey.


Looking forward to an excellent tryout!


Matthew Wheeler
