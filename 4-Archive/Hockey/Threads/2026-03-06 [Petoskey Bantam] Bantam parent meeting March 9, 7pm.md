# [Petoskey Bantam] Bantam parent meeting March 9, 7pm

**1 message** on 2026-03-06

## 1. 2026-03-06 16:22 — no-reply@mailer.sportsengine.com

Petoskey Area Hockey Association

Hello again Bantam families,

Following up on our previous email, we are planning a parent meeting for Monday, March 9 at 7pm primarily to discuss plans for the 2026-27 season.

Spring Hockey
So far we don't have enough interested players to form a team for a spring tournament. We'll talk more on Monday, but if you're interested please fill out the survey. Thanks!


End of the Season Update
Adray State Finals on March 13-15 is the end of our season so Thursday, March 12 will be our last official practice.

We are planning to use our regular Monday ice time March 16 and 23, with the 12U players coming up next season joining us. Obviously our season will be over at that point but if we don't end up having a spring team it will be our last chance to get next season's together. (If we do still need a spring tryout it will be on the 23rd.)

Thursday, March 19, 5:30-8:30pm is the PAHA end of season celebration, with our age group being asked to come from 7-8:30pm so the younger kids can be on the ice first. (Obviously if you have a younger player you can all come at the earlier time.) There will be food, end of season awards, and open ice for the kids to enjoy. We hope you all attend!


Fall 2026-27 Bantam Tryout
We will talk more about this at Monday's parent meeting, but the earliest date MAHA allows us to hold the tryout is May 18. Right now we are looking at Monday, May 18, or Tuesday, May 19 at 7pm pending confirmation with the rink. Pencil that in but stay tuned.

A Note on Tryouts Generally
A few people have asked about trying out for other spring teams. If your player is interested, we are totally OK with them going to tryouts for other teams (either spring hockey or next season). Tryouts are a really good experience for the kids and absolutely no one here will hold it against your player. Plus, a lot of you are coming a long way to Petoskey so we totally understand why you would want to look at your options.

A big reason why we are trying to get organized really early for the 2026-27 season is so you know what's being offered here as you go into the tryout season. So, of course we want you back but we want you to know its ok to go to other tryouts.

Thanks again,
Chris and Brent
