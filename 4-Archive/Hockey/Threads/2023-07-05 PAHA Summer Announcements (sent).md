PAHA Summer Announcements

Good Afternoon, PAHA Families –
We hope summer is treating you well and would like to pass along some important information…
First, we would like to welcome our new board members that were elected into our association.  Please welcome our new members:  Chris Firman and Anita Szczepanski, as well as our returning members: Julianne Imbleau, Elizabeth Morey, and Sarah Saddison.
For board member contact information, meeting minutes, and assigned roles, please see our website: https://www.petoskeyhockey.com/board
We would like to thank our outgoing board members, who will be greatly missed: Michael Heflin and Brian Kasiborski.  A huge thank you for your leadership, time, and dedication towards helping our local kids play hockey.
Second, PAHA relies on volunteer coaches, and with a growing program we need coaches at all age levels! If you're interested, please check that box when you register your player and/or contact our Director of Coaching Brent Bolin, brentcbolin@gmail.com, who can answer your questions and guide you through the USA Hockey certification process. Note that PAHA will cover your costs for joining USA Hockey and getting certified!
Third, PAHA 2023-24 Season Registration is now OPEN!
Early Registrations will receive a $50 discount per player if you register by August 15, 2023.  These discounts apply to all house teams and will be deducted manually from your ice bills.
Please register at your earliest convenience through our registration link: https://petoskeyhockey.sportngin.com/register/form/517733969
Registration costs can be paid in full online or can be paid offline (billed in 3 installments).  The first bill will be sent in October via Sports Engine.  Registration costs will include a Petoskey hockey practice jersey and game socks to keep!
 For more PAHA information and communications, please “like” our Facebook page (Petoskey Area Hockey Association – PAHA) and/or visit our website: www.petoskeyhockey.com
Also, if you know other families interested in hockey that would be new members, please feel free to forward this email to them. It would be greatly appreciated!
Thank you for your time in reading through the summer announcements. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out.
Enjoy the rest of your summer!
-Sarah Saddison,
PAHA Secretary
