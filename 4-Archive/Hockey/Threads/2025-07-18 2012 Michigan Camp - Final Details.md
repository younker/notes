Hey Everyone,

We are excited to meet all of you tomorrow.

Some reminders from the last email:

- Check in will take place at 9 AM in the lobby at Yost on Friday. There is a parking lot between the baseball stadium and the track as you pull in. Park in any of those spots you find available.

- Lunch will be from 1-1:30 each day, players are on their own to bring food. There are many restaurants/take out places close by that parents can pick up food from if you would like.

- There will be an off-ice component run by our strength coach. Please bring work out clothes & shoes.

- We will have a hydration station set up with water and gatorade, so please feel free to bring your own bottle to refill throughout the day.

I made some small tweaks to the schedule and trimmed up some time in between sessions & seminars. Both days will end at 4:30 at the latest.

Schedules will be posted all over the rink so everyone will know whats going on at all times.

Rosters are attached and the schedule is broken down by teams. There might be a final tweak to the rosters by the morning, we have some last minute injuries.

If anyone has any questions, please let me know.

Thanks!
