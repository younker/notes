# Hockey

## Level 3 Class

- Need more than a problem identifier; need a problem solution.
- Kids are boomerangs - coach the next shift on the bench, not the current shift on the ice.
- You cannot ridicule a kid in front of others; you will not help but only harm.
- Value of communication and support.
- They are really smart and good at learning - we just need to get out of their way.
- We need to create environments to allow our kids to create solutions and be creative. We need problem solvers, not robots.
- Takes 10-12 times for a kid to learn something.
- Separate explanation from demonstration.
