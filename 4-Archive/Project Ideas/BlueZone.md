# BlueZone

> [!info] Archived - closed project idea from [[Justin Younker]]. Related to the [[Senior Living Portfolio MOC|Senior Living Portfolio]] (Blue Zone / Costa Rica site).

- Directory of communities.
- Forum for conversations (healthcare, assisted living, visas, currency exchange).

## Content

- Community directory.
- Resources: visas, financial planning, healthcare.
- Community forum.

## Audience

- 65-75 year old who wants to travel with health needs and special requirements - what if something happens (resources)? OR expats already living there who don't want to move back to the USA but need to know their options.
- Healthy baby boomers who want to travel.

## Start With...

- Costa Rica. Directory (nobody else is doing this).
- What are the options for living there as a baby boomer (independent living, golf course, on-site healthcare/nurse)?

## Training Questions for BlueZone Travel

- I would like to live in the Nicoya Peninsula, Costa Rica for an extended time period. What is the maximum amount of time I can live there with a visa?
- If I need to refill prescriptions or need other medical care while visiting, what are my options?
- How is the healthcare system for non-residents?
- What are some housing options for a 3-to-6-month stay in Nicoya?
- What are my options for immersing myself in the culture of this blue zone?
- How safe is the Nicoya peninsula?
- Which airport is best to fly in and out of, and should I rent a car or are there other options?
- How much should I budget for food and housing per day?
- Is there a large language barrier?
- Why is this blue zone known for longevity and wellness?
- If I enjoy this area and would like to move there, how do I obtain residency?
- Are there communities of expats located in this region?
