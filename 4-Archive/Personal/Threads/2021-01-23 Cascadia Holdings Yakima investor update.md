## 1. 2021-01-23 00:51 — investor.relations@cascadiaseniorliving.com

Cascadia Holdings Yakima investors,

We wanted to take this opportunity to update our investors on the sale of Orchardwest, our assisted/independent living building in Yakima.  Over the past 6 months, we've been working to secure HUD financing as a part of the purchase of Orchardwest, Fieldstone Yakima Memory Care, and Fieldstone Adult Family Home from their current majority owners.  Our application was submitted 2 months ago, unfortunately, due to historically low interest rates, turnaround time from HUD is longer than expected.  We hope to receive an assigned underwriter any day now.  This would result in a transaction date this summer 2021.  We will be sure to keep you updated.

As always, we appreciate your investment and please let us know if you have any questions.

Cascadia Holdings Yakima investors,
