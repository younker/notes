Dear Members of the PAHA Board,

I am writing to express my support for Coach Brent Bolin, whose dedication, expertise, and exemplary character have significantly impacted my son and his team, both on and off the ice. As a parent of a PeeWee player this season, I have had the privilege of witnessing Coach Brent's positive influence on the players, fostering their athletic development, and personal growth.

Coach Brent's commitment to the boys is evident in every practice, game, and team event. Under his guidance, our team has shown remarkable improvement and growth, both as a team and as individuals. However, what sets Coach Brent apart is his ability to teach our boys, emphasizing the importance of hard work, teamwork, and perseverance.

Off the ice, Coach Brent has been an outstanding role model for our young athletes. He consistently goes above and beyond to support the players, offering encouragement and advice, and taking a genuine interest in their lives beyond hockey. He spends countless hours every week making sure the boys have a schedule that will challenge them and offer them opportunities for growth. He takes time to listen to concerns and he takes the boy's experiences personally and I have witnessed him following up when concerns are raised on multiple occasions.

A specific example of this was after a game when our son didn’t play as many shifts as he would like. He was upset and to be honest as parents, we were upset seeing him upset. We immediately had a conversation with the coaches and we were able to come up with a plan that included things our son needed to work on. We then encouraged our son to talk to Coach Brent himself. He did this and we saw immediate action on the coach's parts and by our son. Our son worked hard on those things he discussed with the coaches and he got better. This gave our son a chance to be coached, to work to get better at a sport he loves and one he hopes to play in the future, and most importantly, how to advocate for himself. He is 12 years old and this is an important skill to have, especially in competitive sports. Additionally, we saw multiple games where Coach Brent pulled our son aside after the game to give him feedback and positive encouragement. This helped our son grow both as a hockey player and as a young man. In my opinion, this is what a coach does. This is what Coach Brent does and we are grateful he gives so freely of his time and talent.

Moreover, Coach Brent's approach to coaching and mentorship has fostered a positive and inclusive team environment, where players are encouraged to support one another and celebrate each other's successes. This team spirit has not only enhanced the team’s performance but has also created lasting friendships among the players, contributing to a strong and cohesive community within our association.

I have been impressed by Coach Brent’s ability to show grace and rise above efforts to work against the positive environment created and actions to create division among the team and our hockey community. It is my hope that PAHA will take steps to encourage families to set positive examples for our kids and to express concerns as they arise in healthier, more productive ways so we can model good examples of conflict resolution for our children.

In conclusion, Coach Brent’s exemplary leadership and dedication have made a significant impact on our team and community, embodying the values and standards I know PAHA strives to promote. Thank you for taking the time to consider my letter. I am confident that with Coach Brent at the helm, our team will continue to thrive and achieve even greater success in the future.

Sincerely,

Heather Younker
