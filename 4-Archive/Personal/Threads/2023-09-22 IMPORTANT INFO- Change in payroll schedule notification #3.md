# IMPORTANT INFO- Change in payroll schedule notification #3

**1 message** on 2023-09-22

## 1. 2023-09-22 11:44 — liz.white@ziffdavis.com

Happy Friday!

One last reminder for our payroll transition!

Today you will be receiving the *last pay check* under the old bi-weekly
frequency. *On Monday September 25th, *you will receive *another* paycheck
that will cover your wages from *9/17 until the end of the month 9/30.
* After that,
the following scheduled pay will be deposited on *Friday October 13th.*

*For those receiving commission payments:*
Commissions will continue to be paid on first paycheck of each month, which
going forward will be on the *15th* or previous Friday if the 15th falls on
a weekend.

Here are the dates that commissions will be paid for the remainder of the
year:

   - October 13, 2023
   - November 15, 2023
   - December 15, 2023

Please don't hesitate to contact HR if you have any questions and have a
wonderful weekend.

~Liz

>> Happy Friday!

>> In order to align our payroll practices with the rest of the company we
>> will be changing our payroll cycle to a semi-monthly frequency. This means
>> there will be 24 pay periods throughout the calendar year as opposed to the
>> current 26.
>>
>> You are receiving this notice because you *will be* impacted by this
>> change. Effective October 1st, 2023, you will be paid semi-monthly, pay
>> dates will be on the 15th and the last day of the month.
>>
>> It is important that employees plan their personal finances in advance of
>> this transition, as the conversion will directly impact the net pay check.
>> Your take home pay for a semi-monthly period will be a little more than it
>> was on a bi-weekly schedule; however, your *overall compensation is not
>> changing* a*nd you will receive the same annual salary subject to
>> working your usual schedule. *
>>
>> Please see the illustration below on the gross difference between
>> paychecks on a semi-monthly vs a biweekly pay period. We cannot tell you in
>> advance how much your net paycheck will be impacted, because that depends
>> on different factors such as your withholding filing status, benefit
>> deductions, and/or contributions.
>>
>> The last pay date under the old schedule will be on 9/22/2023. We will
>> have an extra pay on 9/25/2023 that will cover the wages from 9/17/2023
>> through 9/30/2023. The first paycheck on the new schedule will be October
>> 13th, and will include the wage period from 10/1/2023 to 10/15/2023.
>>

>> status. Your annual salary, benefits, tax withholdings, working hours,
