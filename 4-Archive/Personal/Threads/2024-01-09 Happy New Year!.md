As you prepare for tax season, it's important to make sure you have the following items readily available for your accountant.


  1.  Closing Disclosure Statement from the Purchase or Refinance of your Home (See attached)
  2.  Closing Statement from the sale of your home - Title or Your REALTOR may provide.
  3.  Mortgage Interest Statement - This will come with your January or February mortgage statement.
  4.  A copy of your Summer and Winter Taxes - This may be obtained on your cities website if not received in the mail.
  5.  A copy of your Homeowners Insurance Declarations page - This may be obtained from your insurance agent.
