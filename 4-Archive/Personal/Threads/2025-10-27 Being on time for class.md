## 1. 2025-10-27 16:31 — kdutcher@harborlight.org

Good afternoon Younker family,

I hope your day is going well! I am writing to let you know that Emily has
reached three unexcused tardies in her first hour class this semester.
According to our school handbook, the consequence for this is a detention
which can be served with me any day this week or next week after school.
Please let me know which day works best for your family.

I understand that mornings and daily routines can sometimes be challenging. I
desire to come alongside families with grace and support. Our goal is to
help students grow in responsibility, and being on time is an important
habit. Building consistency now helps prepare students not only for
academic success but for life.

If there is anything I can do to support you, Emily, in getting to school
and into class on time, or if circumstances are contributing to this that I
should be aware of, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

With gratitude,
Kelly
