Good Morning All,

You can now create your UnitedHealthCare portal access and download digital copies of your membership cards. Please follow the steps below to complete the registration process:

1. Navigate to https://member.uhc.com/<https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/member.uhc.com/__;!!KaA_j8q37Q!EbgKFE84OYI_rCKDKAsTP-K41J1t5GatLp5nDpad2zWBtpxhllPP6bkh-hxMab96PJm9YBqw1s5QQzd8UwKBGIES9UsyEQss$>
2. Select "Register Now"
3. Choose "No, I do not have Medicare" and click "Register Now"
4. Enter First Name, Last Name, and Date of Birth as shown in UKG
5. Under Identification Type, enter SSN without dashes and click Continue

There's also a convenient QR code to download the mobile app and add ID cards to your digital wallets!

For employees receiving care from out-of-network providers, you can now submit the TOC/COC forms. A blank copy of the form is attached to this email. Forms should be submitted directly to UHC at the address or fax number listed on the form.

Physical cards have also been mailed to addresses as shown in UKG and should arrive prior to January 1st, 2026.

Please let me know if you have any questions.
