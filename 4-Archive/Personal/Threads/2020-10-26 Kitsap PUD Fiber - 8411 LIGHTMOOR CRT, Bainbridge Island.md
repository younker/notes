Good afternoon Vivienne,

Thank you for your interest in Kitsap PUD Fiber and your patience in our response.

Please be advise that this is a desktop estimate. Contracts with customers will be based on KPUD’s estimate, but final costs will be based on final construction costs of project. This estimate is valid for 90 days.



We have estimated your Fiber build to be: $20,125  OR  $10,000 if installed at same time as your neighbor at 8415.

To build fiber to your home would require approximately 650’ of aerial and 425’ of underground construction from our nearest connection point on Finch Rd.

KPUD has a minimum connection fee of $2200 which includes the fiber drop to the home up to 1000ft along with electronics, labor & materials for the installation to connect your home. Any additional construction work required to connect your home will be on top of the $2200. The above estimated cost includes the minimum $2200.



KPUD is not a retail service provider. KPUD builds an open access fiber optic system throughout the county and has approved Service Providers who serve customers (express & residential) with reliable high speed internet through the KPUD network.  Currently customers are able to receive 100 Mbps or 1 Gig upload/download speeds & depending on the Service Provider you could also receive Phone &/or TV services. Residents would pay a onetime connection/construction fee to connect your home with fiber & then you would pay your service provider for your monthly service.

To view available Service Providers & Services please visitKPUD.BROADBANDPORTAL.NET.



How do I pay for fiber:

Non-contiguous Local Utility District (NCLUD) allows individual homeowners or a group of homeowners the ability to finance construction costs for utility improvements over a 20 year loan period. These costs are guaranteed with a lien against the property and are repaid with interest annually through the Kitsap County Treasurer’s office.This assessment would be separate than your property taxes. The Treasurer’s office allows homeowners a 30 day period to pay the full amount or portion of final assessment prior to the lien being placed against the property. Interest rates are sitting about 4% & are a fixed rate. Homeowners are able to pay off their final assessment with the Treasurer’s office at any time without penalties or fees. This notarized contract allows KPUD to start construction without any upfront costs from homeowners & the final assessment will be based on the actual costs of construction.



**KPUD encourages residents & their neighbors to complete the KPUD Fiber Survey, to complete the survey please visitKPUD.SERVICEZONES.NET.

These surveys help KPUD understand where customers are interested in service or currently do not have service. This also helps KPUD determine where to build our middle mile fiber with our annual budgeted capital funds along with contact information so we can reach out to customers when fiber has been built near their homes.
