<p>Welcome! Congratulations, Emily has been admitted to Harbor Light Christian School. We are so excited to welcome you to the HLCS family.</p>

<p>We are thankful you are seeking to join a school that will match the philosophy of education that you desire for your family.&nbsp; Harbor Light seeks to be a Christ-centered school that utilizes God&#39;s gifts to support and guide children&#39;s education.&nbsp; We strive, by God&#39;s grace, to be a school that inpacts the entire family and encourages each student to follow God&#39;s calling in their lives.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Just a reminder that the pastor recomendation as well as the referral will need to be completed and emailed back to HLCS before we can finalize acceptance into HLCS.&nbsp; Please check with [[Lisa Veurink|Lisa]] when you call the office for the final interview to make sure all paperwork is in.</p>

<p>The steps for our enrollment process is to complete the following for Emily.</p>

<p><u><strong>Step One:&nbsp; </strong></u>Enrollment Paperwork</p>

<ul>
	<li>Please go to <a href="https://www.factsmgt.com">www.factsmgt.com</a></li>
	<li>Select <strong>Family Log In</strong> from the menu bar and <strong>FACTS Family Portal</strong> from the drop-down menu.</li>
	<li>Type in your username and password. If you have forgotten your username or password, please call the office.</li>
	<li>After logging in, click on <strong>Apply/Enroll</strong> in the left menu.</li>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Enrollment/Reenrollment</strong> link.</li>
</ul>

<p>Our Online Enrollment system will open with a link to the enrollment packet for Emily. The online process should take approximately 15 minutes to complete. Your information will be saved if you need to quit and come back later.</p>

<p>The Instructions and Enrollment Checklist page of the enrollment packet contains supplemental enrollment forms that also must be submitted. Further instructions on these forms are provided online.</p>

<p>If you have any questions about the process, please contact us at 231-347-7859.</p>

<p>Sincerely,</p>

<p>Harbor Light Christian School</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><u>Instructions for Creating a FACTS Family Portal Login <span style="background-color:#f1c40f;">*NEW FAMILIES ONLY</span></u></p>

<ul>
	<li>Please go to <a href="https://www.factsmgt.com">www.factsmgt.com</a>.</li>
	<li>Select <strong>Parent Log in</strong> from the menu bar and <strong>FACTS Family Portal</strong> from the drop-down menu.</li>
	<li>After the FACTS Family Portal Login screen opens, please select <strong>Create New Family Portal Account</strong>.</li>
	<li>Enter (HLC-MI) into the District Code field.</li>
	<li>Enter in the Email field your email address as provided in your application to the school.</li>
	<li>Click the <strong>Create Account</strong> button.</li>
	<li>You will receive an email from FACTS SIS Customer Support containing a link that will allow you to create your username and password. For security purposes the link will remain active for 6 hours.</li>
	<li>Please click on the link. A Change/Create Password screen will open. You may use the default username provided, or create a new username. Then type in your desired password into the Password field and Confirm Field.</li>
	<li>Click on the <strong>Save Username and/or Password</strong> button.</li>
	<li>Close the window.</li>
	<li>Log into Family Portal as instructed above.</li>
</ul>
