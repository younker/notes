We are currently seeking a manager for the white team. Please reach out to Brent Bolin if interested. His email is above.

Please download the SportsEngine app.  You can also access SportsEngine online (sportsengine.com).  Sign in to your account using the same credentials you used at registration.  Once you are logged in, you can click on your team (Petoskey White or Petoskey Blue).  You can then access the practice and game schedule, the roster, use the “chat” feature to talk back and forth with the team and coaches, add photos, etc.  This is where the coaches and managers will put all your game dates and times.  Coaches/Managers will communicate with you through this app.  You can turn on notifications, so that it is like a text feature for you.  Please make sure you go into your schedule and “rsvp” your player for all practices and games. Coaches use this information to plan ahead.

If you would like to volunteer and help with any team duties, please reach out to us.


Thank you!
