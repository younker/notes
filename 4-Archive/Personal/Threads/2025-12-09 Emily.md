# Emily

## 1. 2025-12-09 20:32 — falvarado@harborlight.org

Hello Jason and [[Heather Younker|Heather]],

My name is Felix Alvarado, Emily´s Spanish teacher. I have enjoyed having
her in my class, and I get a kick out of her personality. I am writing you
because I wanted to share with you something that I believe you, as Emily´s
parents, should be aware of.

My wife and I, as Christian Educators, pray over our students regularly. (I
would love to say every night, but to be honest, while my wife does
nightly, I would have to say I do so perhaps every other night.) Now, I do
not know how you will take this, but it is my duty to share with you. From
time to time as I pray over my students, I will receive a word of knowledge
or see a vision relating to a student. I then share (when appropriate) the
word of encouragement or the vision I saw with my students (on a Tuesday
when I am in person at the school). As I said, these words/visions come to
me periodically, and not necessarily pertaining to each student and not
necessarily each week.

Before Thanksgiving, while praying for my students I had a vision for one
of my students, and then when I came to Emily to pray over her, I had a
strong sense that the Holy Spirit was going to show me something, but I
felt as though there was some sort of a block that was preventing it. The
Tuesday before Thanksgiving, I mentioned this to Emily and said that I
would continue to pray over her and seek God regarding this. Later that
week, after several more days of praying and seeking God for Emily, I had a
very strong vision about Emily. I have been holding this for a couple of
weeks now, knowing that your family had traveled during the Thanksgiving
Break. I would like to share it with you here, finally.

While praying over Emily, I saw her standing before me, her right profile.
I saw a vine wrapped around her neck, but not completely around her neck.
It was sort of draped harmlessly over her shoulder. The vine was not
choking her, but it was hanging such that very quickly the vine could be
used to choke her. Though she was not being choked at that moment, I
perceived that it posed a danger to her and that someone or something was
going to try to hurt her. So, I lept to my feet to try to protect her, but
I could not find who or what was threatening her. I was unable to grab the
vine or do anything to remove the threat. Then suddenly, to my right, I saw
a remarkably tall angel. He spoke to me saying, "Tell her that this battle
is not her battle, but the Lord´s, and the battle has already been won. All
she need do is let go." I do not have the interpretation of this vision,
but she and/or you, as her parents, may have wisdom and insight as to the
specific meaning of this vision.

Now, I don´t know if you even believe in or accept such visions. You might
simply think, "Well he seems like a nice guy and well-intentioned, but he
is probably just imagining things." I hope you know that I am, indeed,
well-intentioned and that I care very much for your precious daughter and
all of my students. Furthermore, I take great care with the things of God,
because I would rather die than speak a false word. I only speak when the
Holy Spirit moves, and I know that I know that I know that what I am
hearing or seeing comes from God. But regardless of how you take it or what
you might think of me, it was my responsibility to share this with you. I
trust that as her parents, you will at least take this into prayer to see
if God might be moving in your lives and Emily´s and if this vision has any
meaning that you all can extract from it.

May God give you wisdom and guidance always as you lead and protect your
family. And may He bless the Younker clan extra richly during this special
time of year.

Peace!

Felix Alvarado
