# Cascadia Holdings Yakima Update

**1 message** on 2021-08-03

## 1. 2021-08-03 20:51 — investor.relations@cascadiaseniorliving.com

Cascadia Holdings Yakima investors,

We wanted to take this opportunity to update you on the sale of Orchardwest, our assisted/independent living building in Yakima.

In our last update, we wrote that we were in the middle of securing HUD financing as a part of the purchase of Orchardwest, Fieldstone Yakima Memory Care, and Fieldstone Adult Family Home.  We are still in the middle of the underwriting process and do not expect formal approval until mid-September.  We intend to close this by year-end so please plan this accordingly with your tax professional.

We are excited to announce that Orchardwest has consistently increased its occupancy percentage in 2021.
As of 7/31/21, it reached 85% occupancy.
This is the highest occupancy % it has seen in over 12 months.  This is welcomed news as increasing census in 2020 was extremely difficult due to state-mandated restrictions on tours and family visits.

As always, we appreciate your investment and please let us know if you have any questions.
