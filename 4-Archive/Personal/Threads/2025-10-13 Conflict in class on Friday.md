## 1. 2025-10-13 12:17 — kdutcher@harborlight.org

Good afternoon Heather and Jason,

I hope you are having an enjoyable Monday. I wanted to reach out regarding
an incident that occurred Friday involving Emily. She became upset in class
after another student touched her pencil/pen, and she responded by calling
the student an inappropriate name three times. He responded by calling her
an inapporpriate name as well.

After this incident, she calmed down in the bathroom, then spent time in
Mrs. Barnes’ office talking with Mrs. Barnes and me, and calming down more,
as she felt too embarrassed to return to class that hour.

I want to commend her for being honest about what she said. Because of the
incident, she served a lunch detention with me today. I had her and the
other student join me for lunch today, and we had a brief, constructive
conversation about the situation. We talked about appropriate ways to
express frustration and how to handle conflicts respectfully in the future.

Emily seemed reflective and understood the importance of using kind words,
even when upset. We will continue to support her as she practices these
skills.

Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns.

Warmly,
Kelly Dutcher

In Christ,
