# Referee Incident - Dec. 16, 2022

**1 message** on 2022-12-19

## 1. 2022-12-19 14:51 — brentcbolin@gmail.com

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to document an instance of unacceptable referee conduct that
occurred Friday, December 16, 2022, 6pm Squirts game at Petoskey Ice Arena.
I am the head of Petoskey Squirts White and we were playing against
Petoskey Squirt Blue.

Referee Jim Horn was alone that night, by agreement with PAHA. Myself and
the other White coaches questioned a few calls, and the referee angrily
told us to keep our mouths shut. Early in the second period, I was standing
right on our offensive blue line when the referee whistled our team for
offsides. Not seeing any player offsides because of my point of view, I
simply asked "who was offside?" At this point the referee skated over to
our bench and yelled at me, and I quote: "You need to shut your fucking
mouth" and threatened to kick me out of the gave and assess a penalty
against the team. I responded by pointing out that he just dropped the F
bomb in front of 12 9-10 year old players and he responded "And it was my
pleasure."

This conduct is absolutely unacceptable. I don't care how angry the ref was
at the coaches, there is no excuse whatsoever for having a meltdown and
dropping an F bomb in front of my bench.

Sadly it gets worse. After the game, while signing score sheets at the
opposing bench, the ref said something to the effect of "I don't get to
tell people to fuck off at my day job but I get to do it here at the rink."
This was relayed by Petoskey Blue Head Coach Mike Imbleau, who was taken
aback because he of course had no idea what had occurred on our bench or
why the referee would make such a statement. Coach Imbleau subsequently
asked me what was going on, and when I explained the situation he was
similarly appalled that the ref would swear in front of the kids in that
way.

Ironically this referee was well known to myself and the other White
coaches for his poor attitude, over reactions, and tendency to yell at
coaches due to past experiences. We therefore made a point to watch what we
said and how we said it, which makes this meltdown all the more surprising.
I fully admit we did not sit in silence but I do not believe any of our
conduct warranted a bench warning, let alone an outburst, and that *the
referee's actual reaction was unacceptable* no matter what we had done.

I know we have a national shortage of referees, and that, like all
employment problems, it is worse in Northern Michigan, but I believe this
situation requires a serious response and that at a minimum referee Jim
Horn should not be working youth games.

Thank you,
Brent Bolin
Petoskey Squirts White Heach Coach
PAHA Director of Coaching
