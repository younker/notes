# Alaska Fish and Game license: Order number 2768958

**1 message** on 2021-06-21

## 1. 2021-06-21 11:34 — adfg.license@alaska.gov

Thank you for using the Alaska Department of Fish & Game online store! Attached you will find your item(s).


 If your license is not electronically signed, you must physically sign your license. You must carry the license in your possession whenever you are engaged in the licensed activity (sport fishing, hunting, etc.) and show it upon request to any person authorized to inspect it.


 Please review your information on the attached items(s). If there are any mistakes or if you have any questions, please email adfg.license@alaska.gov or call (907)465-2376.


 Thank you for your investment in Alaska's fish and wildlife! When you buy a license, tag, or permit, the money you spend directly supports ADF&G's management and research of Alaska's fish and wildlife resources. Together, we ensure the future of our sport and commercial fishing, hunting, trapping, subsistence opportunities, and wildlife viewing.
