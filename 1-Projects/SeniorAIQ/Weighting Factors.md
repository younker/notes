# Senior Housing Community Weighting Factors

Part of [[SeniorAIQ]] — early product logic for scoring communities. See also [[AIQ-scoring-summary]].

Factors for evaluating what makes a senior housing community great.

## Priority Order

1. **Manager** - First priority
2. **State Survey Results** - Second priority
3. **Customer Reviews** - Third priority
4. Remaining sections (Location, Owner, Community Leadership, Physical Plant, Price) - To be prioritized based on research
