# Original Text

## Do AI agents get referenced in ChatGPT results?

**Short answer**: Not by default.

AI agents (custom GPTs, external agents, wrappers, etc.) are not automatically referenced or credited by ChatGPT in responses — even if those agents help generate or influence results.

However, there are specific situations where agents can show up or be cited in ChatGPT output, depending on how the system is built.

## How ChatGPT Generates Answers Today
1. Processes input
2. Predicts a response
3. Does not ordinarily show tools/agents involved

No default attribution system.

When AI agents CAN be referenced
- Standard ChatGPT response → No attribution
- Connected agent/tool used → Sometimes
- Custom GPT with retrieval or tools → Yes if configured
- Developer chooses to expose logs → Full visibility

Attribution is opt-in, not automatic.

Relevance to the Senior Housing Directory

AI will not naturally show your agent unless:
1. It is integrated as a tool
2. Your dataset is unique and needed
3. Users request it directly
4. Your system becomes authoritative

## How to get your AI agent referenced

AI agents are NOT automatically referenced in ChatGPT.
