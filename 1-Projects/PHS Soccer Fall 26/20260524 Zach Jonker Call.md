## Summer Camp

- Hope College camp was his pick. Coach contact: **Dave**.
- Format: 3 days (evening arrival -> morning/evening x 2 days). Brought coaches in July for a mini camp.
- Day structure: 90 min morning, 90 min afternoon, scrimmage at night.
- Lodged + ate at the university (breakfast / lunch / dinner).
- Cost: ~$300/kid; booster money dropped it to ~$200. Today likely closer to **~$400/kid**.
- Trade-offs:
  - Expensive
  - Transportation + chaperones (parents) required
  - Tough for working kids to get off
  - A lot to set up
- Zach only did one year of the overnight format.
