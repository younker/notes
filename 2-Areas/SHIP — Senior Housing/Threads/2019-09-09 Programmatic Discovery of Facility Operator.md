Hello Michael,

Thank you for your response. The FacilityPOC field gives me an individual who is the point of contact for what I assume is the facility operator (and not necessarily the owner). However:
a) I am unsure that this assumption will always be true
b) The POC is an individual, not the operating business/company
c) The POC can be outdated

For example, if you search for FacilityName "Fieldstone Memory Care", you will see the FacilityPOC is "Glessner, Shannon “. Shannon was the executive director for the owner/operator at the time of filing but has since moved on. Additionally, the owner and operator are now distinct entities so I am left to assume she worked for the operator. Lastly, even if it the data was current, I do not know of any mechanism to determine who Shannon was working for (aka the operator).

You can see how these will make programmatic discovery of the operator difficult and error prone. Given all of this, is there another mechanism by which to find the facility operating business name, uid or some other unique identifying information?

Once again, thank you.

Jason Younker
