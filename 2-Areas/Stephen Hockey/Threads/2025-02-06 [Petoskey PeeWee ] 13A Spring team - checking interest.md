Hello parents,If you're getting this email, your player is moving up to Bantams next season (which starts April 1 for USA Hockey purposes). Coaches are hoping to put together a 13A Spring team for an April tournament so the kids can get their feet wet at the next level. We don't have all the details yet but I am reaching out now to put this on your radar and gauge interest.Our team is obviously having a very good season and we would love to keep them playing together as long as possible, plus get ready for the 25-26 season. The 2012 birth year group is big enough that we can have a team with just our kids if everyone plays.Petoskey Ice Arena is melting the ice April 1 to do maintenance ahead of Fed Camp this summer, so we will be looking for ice availability in Gaylord and Kalkaska. Depending on what we can get, I am thinking we would practice twice a week the first 3 weeks of April and once the week of April 21. Tournament weekend would be April 25-27 in the Metro Detroit area (there are a few options that weekend).Cost per player will depend on how much practice ice we end up getting, but my estimated cost for a tryout, 7 practices, and the tournament is about $330 per kid with 13 players.I know a lot of our kids play a different sport in the spring and we will work around that as much as possible in terms of schedule, as long as you can make the tournament weekend. Just keep me up to date as your learn more about your spring schedule.At this point I'm just asking for you to reply to this email with a Yes/Maybe/No about this spring team so we can gauge interest and start planning. Please also reach out with any questions you have!Thanks,Brent
