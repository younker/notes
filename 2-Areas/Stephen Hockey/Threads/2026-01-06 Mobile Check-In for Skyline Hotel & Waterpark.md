We would like to take this opportunity to advise you of our new Mobile Check-In and Mobile Check-Out feature for your stay at Skyline Hotel & Waterpark.

Our mobile arrival and departure process now gives you more control over your experience and billing before and after your stay.  On your day of arrival please stay tuned to your email and mobile phone for email/text message communications which will initiate the automated process.  This will provide you with the opportunity to confirm or change your method of payment, confirm your billing details, and input information that would previously have been obtained by us during the in person check in process.  We kindly urge you to make use of this new feature as it is designed to streamline and quicken the check-in process to save all of our guests valuable time.

On your day of checkout you will again receive an email/text message link to view and process your final bill and register your departure from the room.

We thank you in advance for making use of our new enhanced Mobile Platform.

Require Parking? Self-parking is available for $23.00 per night, per vehicle plus mandatory Local Fee (7.9% taxable), plus taxes
