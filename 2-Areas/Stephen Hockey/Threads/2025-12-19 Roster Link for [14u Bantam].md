Dear Coach FIRMAN,
You are receiving this email since you are currently listed as the Head
Coach on the roster of 14u Bantam.

 As the Head Coach, you are responsible for knowing the players and fellow
coaches/staff that are on the Official USA Hockey roster. Please review
your roster periodically to make sure that all the players that are playing
in games for this team are on the official roster and that all coaches that
are on the bench or will be on the bench are also on the roster. If you
need to add a player or coach before a game, they must also be added to the
official roster for that game. You will need to contact your Local Program
Registrar before the game to add any new players or coaches or drop
individuals from the roster.

*For National Tournament Bound Rosters*:

   - All players must be added to roster by 12/31 or if a state/district
   tournament is prior to 12/31, no roster changes will be accepted after that
   date
   - All players must have positions and jersey numbers assigned
   - Official roster must list Locker Room Monitor(s), minimum of 1 required
   - All coaches must be added to official roster at least 7 days prior to
   National Championships
   - Game count requirement must be met prior to State/Affiliate/Districts
   or Nationals; requirement is 20/10 youth, 14/10 Girls, 10/5 High School

If you have any questions regarding your roster, please reach out to your
Local Program Registrar.  Have a great season!
