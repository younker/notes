# Hartland Hockey Club Spring Tryouts March 16-20

**1 message** on 2026-03-15

## 1. 2026-03-15 19:15 — notifications@hartlandhockey.club

﻿Hello Parents,This is a quick reminder for families who have registered for tryouts that your player is scheduled to be on the ice this week. Please be sure to confirm your times below. Please plan to arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled ice time so players have enough time to check in, get dressed, and be ready for the start of their session.A few reminders for this week:• Please check in at the registration table when you arrive to receive a tryout jersey.• Locker room assignments and additional instructions will be posted on the monitors in the lobby.• If your player is unable to attend, please notify us as soon as possible.• Tryout jerseys are to be turned in after each skate.Tryouts are an exciting time for the club and we look forward to seeing all the players compete and showcase their skills.If you have any last-minute questions please reach out.Best of luck to all players this week!Best regards,Josh SchafferHartland Hockey Club
