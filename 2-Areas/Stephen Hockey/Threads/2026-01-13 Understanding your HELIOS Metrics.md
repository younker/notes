Hustle Score & Rankings by Birth Year

DID YOU KNOW?

Your Hustle Score and Rankings by Birth Year are powered by a personalized performance model built from your first 5–10 sessions. It's important to use these sessions to set your baseline to improve against as through your own development.

Once the HELIOS App has enough data, it automatically starts showing your Hustle Score for each session and compares your performance to others in your birth year. If you’re seeing ‘--’ for now, it just means the model is still learning you—but it's getting there fast.

You can find Rankings by Birth Year under the Core Skills section in the Skills tab. These rankings update nightly, using your Game and Practice data to show exactly where you stand compared to players your age.

It’s all about helping you understand your strengths, target your weaknesses, and take your game to the next level.

NOTE : We only rank you based on performance from Game and Practice sessions. Skills session are excluded.
