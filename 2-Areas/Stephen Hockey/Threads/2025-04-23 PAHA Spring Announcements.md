## 1. 2025-04-23 15:22 — noreply@mailer.sportsengine.com

PAHA Spring Announcements

Good Morning, PAHA Families-
We hope everyone is recovering from the ice storm and excited for summer!  As we look ahead to our 2025-26 hockey season, we have a couple quick announcements to share.
We currently have open board positions that are up for renewal next season. If you have any interest in joining the board of directors, please email and we will add your name to the ballot. Email nominations with a short biography to: pahacommunications@gmail.com  (Deadline: Friday, May 9, 2025)
If you have any interest in Coaching (or Assistant Coaching) any of our PAHA teams for this next season, please email your interest to: pahacommunications@gmail.com as soon as possible.
The 2025-26 season registration will be opening very soon, so stay tuned for details on that.
If any families have brand new game jerseys or borrowed equipment still out, please finalize turning it in to our Equipment Manager, Doug Nowels. His email is: dnowels@reopetoskey.com
Finally, some of our parents have borrowed old game adult jerseys that were worn to cheer our teams on. If you are interested in keeping the old game jerseys, you may do so. If you are keeping the game jersey(s), we are asking for a donation to be made for the payment (your choice on the amount).  Donations should be written in the form of a check to: PAHA and in the memo section, please write old game jersey donation.  Since the rink is closed, please mail your game jersey donations to: PAHA, PO BOX 862, Petoskey, MI 49770.  If you would like to return your old game jerseys, please contact Doug at his email address above.
We will keep in touch on future updates.  If you have any questions, please reach out!
Thank you,
-Sarah, PAHA Secretary
