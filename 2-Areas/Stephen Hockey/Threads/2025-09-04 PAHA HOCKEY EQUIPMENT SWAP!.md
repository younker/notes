# PAHA HOCKEY EQUIPMENT SWAP!

**2 messages** from 2025-09-04 to 2025-09-04

## 1. 2025-09-04 19:59 — pahacommunications@gmail.com

Past, Present, and Future PAHA Families,

Join us on *Wednesday, September 11th, 2025 from 6:00–8:00 PM* at the
Petoskey Ice Arena for our *Hockey Equipment Swap & PAHA Stick & Puck*!

It’s the perfect time to dig into your closets, basements, and garages and
pull out any gently used gear your players have outgrown. Bring equipment
to exchange, sell, or donate, and help another skater get on the ice this
season.

***We’re especially in need of larger goalie gear to support our growing
12U and 14U teams.***

Every piece makes a difference!

Stick & Puck will be open to current PAHA players

Questions on equipment? Contact [[Doug Hughes|Doug]] Nowels at dnowels@reopetoskey.com or
pahacommunications@gmail.com
--
