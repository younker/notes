# Senior Living Portfolio

Top-level Map of Content for the Senior Living Portfolio — a three-site WordPress build owned with my brother Justin Younker (Cascadia Development). Lead-gen + monetization platform targeting US/Canadian retirees researching retirement abroad, with Blue Zone Senior Living as the flagship and Panama / Costa Rica as geo sites.

Long-term vision: passive income via affiliate partnerships and directory listings now, audience + authority to support future senior living community developments in Costa Rica and Panama later.
