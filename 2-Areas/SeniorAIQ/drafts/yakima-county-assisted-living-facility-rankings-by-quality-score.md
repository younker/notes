We scored 26 assisted living facilities in Yakima County using public data from DSHS inspection reports and popular review sites. Here's how they rank.

## How We Score

Each facility gets a composite quality score (0-100) built from multiple data sources including:
- DSHS inspection history
- Review ratings
- Property Assessments
- Proximity to points-of-interest (eg hospitals and pharmacies)
- Operator Track Record

Inspection reports are scored on both violation severity and facility response quality -- because how a place handles problems matters as much as whether they have them.[^1]

## What Stood Out

### 1. Highgate Senior Living

Highgate's 4.8-star Google rating across 80 reviews is the highest of any assisted living facility in Yakima County.[^2] The county average is 4.24 stars. Every review posted in the past year gave 5 stars.[^3] That volume and consistency is what puts Highgate at #1.

The inspection record backs it up. DSHS records show two scored inspections since March 2024.[^4] The more notable one flagged care planning gaps for 5 of 7 sampled residents -- incomplete assessments, missing behavioral intervention plans, and delayed physician notifications.[^5] A serious finding. But the facility corrected everything within seven weeks and passed follow-up with zero remaining deficiencies.[^5] The more recent September 2025 fire safety inspection came back clean.[^4] That pattern -- problem identified, problem fixed quickly -- is exactly what the response score is designed to capture.

Highgate is also within a quarter mile of both a pharmacy and a grocery store, and operates as a single, locally owned facility rather than part of a chain.[^6][^7]

### 2. Fieldstone OrchardWest

Fieldstone OrchardWest earns the #2 spot with a 4.6-star Google rating and strong location access.[^2] It's a quarter mile from a hospital and under half a mile from the nearest pharmacy.[^6] The reviews lean heavily toward long-term family members. One wrote that their parents had lived there for over three years and described it as a place where residents are "safe and well taken care of daily."[^8]

OrchardWest is part of a four-facility portfolio under the same operator.[^7] The group's median quality score of 75 across all four locations suggests consistent management rather than one standout property.

### 3. The Chesterley

The Chesterley's recent review trend is the strongest in the top five. Its 4.4-star Google rating comes from 20 reviewers, and the three most recent posts -- all within the last two years -- are unanimous 5-star ratings.[^2] One came from a registered nurse who opened with, "As an RN with years of experience, I felt the need to review this place," before going on to describe the care her mother received.[^9] When healthcare professionals go out of their way to leave feedback, that's a data point worth flagging.

It also sits closer to a hospital than any other top-10 facility -- under a tenth of a mile -- with a pharmacy about half a mile away.[^6]

The Chesterley and the #5-ranked Cascades of Chesterley share the same campus and operator, both running 96 beds under a two-facility group with a median quality score of 76.[^7][^10]

### 4. Fieldstone Memory Care

Fieldstone Memory Care is the specialized sibling to #2-ranked OrchardWest, purpose-built for memory care with 58 beds. At 4.3 stars across 29 Google reviews, it's above the county average but shows a wider spread than its peers -- from detailed 5-star accounts praising attentive care to a longer 1-star review describing a difficult family experience.[^2][^11] That kind of range is common in memory care, where patient complexity and family expectations vary more than in general assisted living.

Location is a strength. The facility is a quarter mile from a hospital, under half a mile from both a pharmacy and a grocery store, and half a mile from a park.[^6] The property's $11.76 million assessed value -- nearly three times the county median -- reflects the specialized build-out that memory care requires.[^12] Like OrchardWest, it's managed by the same four-facility operator group.[^7]

### 5. Cascades of Chesterley

Cascades rounds out the top five as the campus partner to The Chesterley (#3). Its 4.4-star Google rating matches its neighbor, and both share the same operator.[^2][^7]

What separates them by a single rank: The Chesterley picked up three fresh 5-star reviews in the last two years, giving it a small edge in review recency. Cascades also sits slightly farther from the nearest pharmacy -- half a mile versus a third of a mile -- which nudges the location profile lower.[^6]
