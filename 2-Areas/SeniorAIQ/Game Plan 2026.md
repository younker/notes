# SeniorAIQ Game Plan 2026

Strategic plan for [[SeniorAIQ]] from [[Justin Younker]], outlining ownership structure, Phase 1 launch (2026-09-01), pricing, execution, and Phase 2 ideas.

## Ownership Structure

All assets owned by **TBD, LLC**.

- **Jason — 50%** — contributing time, knowledge, and skills to build out products
- **TBD, LLC — 50%** — contributing time, knowledge, and cash to build out product

## Phase 1 — Launch 2026-09-01

**Scope:** Washington State only.

### Products & Pricing

| Product | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Consumer report — Standard | $49 | AL or MC |
| Consumer report — Premium | $99 | With operator intel |
| Operator State Report | $250 | Details + comparison of all state-licensed communities for a specific operator |
| Market Comparison Report | $250 | Details + comparison of all AL & MC in a specific market |

### Volume & Revenue Projection (consumer reports)

- 3,000 – 5,000 families touring/researching per month
- 10% adoption rate → ~400 sales/month
- Revenue: **low $19,600 / high $39,600** per month

## Execution

### Frame the value correctly

- **Transparency** — help families make informed decisions
- Senior housing is a huge cost; a $99 investment to support that decision is wise

### Marketing — SEO, paid search, social media, AI discoverability

- Market all WA markets similar to how [[Cascadia Senior Living]] is marketed
- Build out per-market pages for the entire state
  - URL pattern example: `senioraiq.com/washington/yakima/assistedliving`
  - **Open question:** what info on those pages is most valuable?
- Continue blogs per [[blog-content-strategy]]
  - Avoid giving away info that is also being sold in the reports
  - Specific blog ideas: "What is memory care", upcoming certification and licensing changes

### Create Trust

- Good UI / website (visual, content, disclosures)
- Strong report formats
- Useful comparisons

## To Do List

1. Add real estate transaction date (county assessor data) so we know when the underlying real estate changed owners
2. Have legal counsel review for proper disclosures, wording, etc.
3. Create the brand
4. Review and fine tune prompts and scoring — see [[AIQ-scoring-summary]]

## Phase 2 Ideas

- **Recommendation engine** — gather information from user (need, location, pricing, etc.) and recommend matches
- **2nd state launch** — likely California
- **International** — informational products on senior housing in Costa Rica and Panama
- **Intelligence reports**
  - For **operators** — possible barter: pricing, updated state reports, and other info in exchange for intel
  - For **investors, insurance, owners, other stakeholders** — TBD
