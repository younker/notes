## Concession Stand

[[Meg Mainland]] runs the concession stand for the stadium. Email the team at the start of the season for each family to sign up for one game.

**Contact:** [[Meg Mainland]] -- 231-330-3348 -- meganmainland@gmail.com

### Health-department volunteer requirements
- Closed-toed shoes only -- no sandals
- No tank tops or sleeveless shirts
- Wash hands on entry, after touching garbage/hair/bathroom, before gloving, between glove changes
- Hair pulled back OR hats/hair nets (extras available); includes men's beards
- No children in concession unless authorized by [[Meg Mainland]]
- No backpacks or kids' items stored in concession -- send to vehicle
- Volunteer coordinators handle breaks
- No eating in concession except designated area; drinks must have lids

### Operations
- Meg turns on coffee, hot cocoa, warmers; makes coffee; fills candy boxes
- Volunteers handle hot dogs, popcorn, etc. (Meg may prep if time allows -- not guaranteed)
- If a volunteer leaves mid-shift, coach/volunteer coordinator must cover or concession shuts down
- Every team responsible for full-game coverage including setup and cleanup
- High schoolers OK as fillers (usually NHS students) but at least 1-2 adults must be present besides Meg
- Meg reserves right to remove anyone for behavior issues
- All items inventoried end of night; invoice sent to boosters/coaches

Questions: [[Joel Dohm]] or [[Meg Mainland]] (231-330-3348 / meganmainland@gmail.com)

## Snacks / Waters for Bus

Sign-up for parents to drop off healthy snacks and drinks before each away-game bus leaves. Notify parents of any allergies.
