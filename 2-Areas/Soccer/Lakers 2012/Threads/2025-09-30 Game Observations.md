*Backline*

   - Playing 3 in the back lends itself to potential 3v3s.
      - Do you want the wingers to come back and provide cover when
      necessary?
   - Tough to build from here with only 3.
   - Need to step up quick as a group to compress space when team
   transitions forward
   - Would like to see [[Carter|Carter]] build or play the keeper rather than just
   play a ball out a couple of times.

*Mids*

   - I believe that [[Graham|Graham]] W. and [[Roland|Roland]] W. are two of the stronger players
   in the group.  They get lost in the number of bodies in the midfielder.
   Lots of bodies and not a lot of space.  Can we create more space for them
   to do their thing?
   - Rarely switch the ball, team is narrow
   - What is the plan when we get close to goal?  We kept possession but
   struggled with ideas.  Services without purpose or poor technique
      - Should one player go 1v1?
      - Play the ball out wide for a cross/service
   - Can we find opportunities to switch the point of attack, needs to
   begin with positioning and then game awareness

*Frontline*

   - Take players on close to goal, generate chances, earn corner kicks.
   etc..
