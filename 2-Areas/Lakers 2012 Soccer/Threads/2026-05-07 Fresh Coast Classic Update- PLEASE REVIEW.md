Fresh Coast Classic, taking place May 29-May 31st, 2026. We are excited to
welcome your team to a weekend of competitive soccer, sportsmanship, and
community along Northern Michigan’s beautiful Fresh Coast.

closer to the tournament date. If you have any questions in the meantime,
our tournament staff is happy to assist. We appreciate your commitment and
look forward to seeing your team on the field later this month!
