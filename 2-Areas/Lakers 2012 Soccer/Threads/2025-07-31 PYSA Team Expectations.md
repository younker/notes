Jason,

   - I will send my lesson plan for the day by early afternoon so you have
   a chance to review it before this evening.
   - Let's plan to tag team the meeting.  I will bring copies of
   expectations and review them in-person and speak and also make a comment
   about player and parent behaviors on the sidelines. Feel free to take it
   from there.
   - This week I will focus on individual and small group defending.
   Building a foundation for the season.
      - Getting down names and jumping in where you are able is an
      excellent objective for the evening.
      - I am certainly thinking having an objective for the team to focus
      on is a good starting point. I am also unsure what this group will really
      look like when they take the field as they really are a new team.
      - Our 2012s always played up and the games that I viewed I thought
      they needed work on moving together as a unit. At times there
was a lot of
      kick and chase.  A starting point is providing a clear definition of what
      we want to do when we have the ball (move the ball forward, spread out,
      play to feet, look to score) and what we want to do when we do
not have the
      ball (apply immediate pressure, provide defensive support/cover,
anticipate
      the next play).  It is broad but team shape when we defend and team shape
      when we attack and moving as a team in both phases would be two specific
      areas that I would like to see the group improve on this fall
      - My main club goal for this age is that players develop skills and
      abilities to contribute to their high school team if they so desire.
      Specific skills where development is needed as a whole.
         - Decision Making
         - Ball Striking
         - Heading
         - 1v1s (both attacking and defending)
            - I think getting feedback from the players on an area where
            they want to improve would be helpful, possibly providing
notecards tonight
            and asking for them back tomorrow.

Everything being stated, we may need to reassess after we see the players
this week.

I think that covers it for the moment.  Let me know if you have any
questions.
