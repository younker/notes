# Petoskey Grassroots Courses 7v7 and 11v11

**1 message** on 2025-07-18

## 1. 2025-07-18 12:00 — bryan.l.goyings@gmail.com

Coaches,

Hello, my name is Bryan Goyings and I will be the lead instructor for the
Grassroots 7v7/11v11 Courses scheduled for Petoskey, MI on Tuesday 7/22 and
Thursday 7/24.

As you review this message, please note that we have some coaches
registered for only the 7v7 portion, another group only registered for the
11v11 portion, and the largest cohort registered for both.  Please note
that there is information provided for both classes in this message and if
you are only planning to attend one class, please review the message
specific to the information that applies to your registration.

*Location:*
PYSA Click Road Field Complex
2325 Click Rd.,
Petoskey, MI 49770

*Schedule:*
5:15-6:30pm Lecture
6:30-8pm Field Session
8-9:15pm Field Session Reflection

Each evening class will begin promptly at 5:15pm and run until 9:15pm.
Please make every effort to be on time as there is a lot of information
that we need to cover.

*Teaching Space*
We will meet under the facility pavilion, just north of the concession
building.  First we will meet for lecture and then head to the field for a
demo session.

*Pre-Course Work:*
When you have a moment please complete the Introduction to Safe and Healthy
Playing Environments module and Safesport training found under the
"Assignments" tab. The final assignment (Page of Experience) will need to
be completed after the course.  I would encourage bringing a notepad or
notebook to take notes on during the meeting.  We have players scheduled to
help with the training session demonstrations so we will not need any
coaches to actively play but we will need several individuals to assist
with the portions of practice coaching.

I look forward to working with everyone next week. If you have any further
questions please do not hesitate to let me know.

Bryan Goyings
US Soccer Grassroots
