# PYSA Early August Update

**1 message** on 2025-08-05

## 1. 2025-08-05 20:00 — bryan@petoskeysoccer.com

Good Afternoon!
A couple club updates as the upcoming soccer season quickly approaches.


Sprocket Sports
If you have not already, be sure you are using our App, Sprocket Sports.  This is the best way to stay up to date on all club and team communications.


Field Opening, Sunday, August 10th 11am-1pm

This is our club wide volunteer event to prepare the fields and complex for the upcoming season.
We ask that one family member from each PYSA family is able to attend. There are a wide variety of tasks that will need attention, no experience necessary.

If a member of your family is unable to attend, you are away for the weekend vacation or have a prior commitment please reach out to Bryan@petoskeysoccer.com and we can coordinate another time or task to help prepare for the upcoming season.

Kick-Off Camp/Club Kick-Off

All premier ages (statewide travel league) begin practice this week, Good Luck!
Next week we have a kick-off camp for all of our players U12 and younger

5:30-6:45pm
Boys and Girls

6:45-8:15pm
Boys and Girls
U9 and U10 Kick-off Camp Runs Monday-Thursday
U11-U12 Kick-off Camp Runs Monday-Thursday

The final day of the Kick-Off Camp is Thursday, 8/14 and corresponds with the PYSA CLUB KICK-OFF (Annual General Meeting).  We want to celebrate all of the players and family that make PYSA a great organization.  U12 teams and younger will have scrimmages that correspond with their training time for camp that week.  In addition we will also have

Variety of fun soccer activities.
PYSA Gear Swamp

Cleats

The week of 8/17, team practices will officially begin for all teams

Your son or daughter's coach will reach out in the near future if they have not done so already with the team's practice schedule
There is a scheduling meeting on Sunday 8/10 for our coaches, after this meeting, each team's coach/manager will work to post a finalized game schedule in the team's calendar.

Thank you for taking the time to review this message.  We are looking forward to an excellent soccer season in the weeks ahead.
