Match fixtures are not generated and public until Friday, July 18 - do not
attempt to begin scheduling with opponents *until scheduling goes live on
July 18 *as divisions & opponents could still potentially change.

A communication will go out once scheduling has begun on July 18 - at this
time fixtures will be available as well as the ability to schedule matches
in GotSport.

-Divisions are built ideally with 8 to 10 teams, using a round robin style
format.
-Divisions with 11 teams (10 matches) are avoided.
-Divisions with 5 or 6 teams are avoided where possible.
 -There is a focus to have divisions with round-robin style play (play each
opponent once), rather than home & away (5 & 6 team divisions). Divisions
with 5 or 6 teams may see additional adjustment to provide a division with
8-10 teams.

 Divisions with 5 teams will play 8 matches (4 home / 4 away - home &
away).
 Divisions with 8 teams will play 7 matches (either 4 home / 3 away - round
robin, or 3 home / 4 away - round robin).
 Divisions with 9 teams will play 8 matches (4 home / 4 away - round
robin).
 Divisions with 10 teams will play 9 matches (either 5 home / 4 away -
round robin, or 4 home / 5 away - round robin).

-The Divisioning Meeting serves as an option for teams to further request
their division placement.
-The Divisioning Meeting is not required to attend if your club / team is
content with placement(s).
-Clubs are suggested to have a representative (suggested 1-2+, such as DOC,
club admin / staff, coach, manager) that will attend the meeting and
attempt to facilitate any needed changes on behalf of the club’s team(s).
-If you have any issues regarding your pre-division placement, contact your
attending club representative or email MSPSP.
-If you are unable to attend and have a request, please contact MSPSP via
email regarding your request.
 -For those who are content with placements and do not attend - it is
*highly unlikely* your teams will see any additional movement unless
required due to division movement / restructures / merges.
-The meeting takes about an hour and a half. Starting with the U13 Boys
we'll make our way through the divisions to the U15 Boys, then U13 Girls to
U18/19 Girls.

 *Scheduling Reminders
 *MSPSP Scheduling will take place several days after the Divisioning
Meeting once changes have been facilitated and fixtures generated. On
Friday July 18th fixtures will become public for teams to see their home
and away matches for the fall season and begin scheduling with opponents.

 In preparation for scheduling, it is essential that teams have the correct
coaches or managers listed on rosters and that the information in the
coach’s user profile (phone number and email) is up to date for other
teams to contact you. To roster coaches or managers, they must have
approved RM requirements.

Scheduling will begin on Friday, July 18.

 Keep an eye out for an MSPSP communication about the scheduling process
going live, scheduling suggestions and tips for managing travel, how to
deal with unresponsive opponents, and other general FAQ.

 *Scheduling Dates
*-MSPSP Scheduling takes place July 18 – July 28
 -Schedules must be entered into GotSport by July 28
 -Schedule Verification takes place July 29 - July 31, during this time
scheduling can still be done and editing / changes can still be made
 -On Aug 1 schedules are final and any reschedules are subject to the
reschedule process

*Next Steps*...
Following the divisioing meeting, the scheduling phase will begin and last
until July 31. Early August we begin to shift to 'pre-season' with many
tournaments and the start of the season.

We will communicate info related to the optional *Fall Kickoff Meetings* (2
