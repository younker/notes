One of my goals as a coach educator is to expose as many coaches as we
can to these courses. Better coaches = better players. Certainly the
course caters to all coaches but one of the target audiences is entry
level and recreational coaches. If you know someone that would value
the course, please share your experience. Players that are engaged and
enjoy the game at a younger age will ideally create a larger pool of
developed players for older age groups and high school teams. That is
just one of the reasons why we need positive and intelligent coaching
at the younger ages.
