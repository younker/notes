# Lakers 2012 Spring Schedule Notes

**5 messages** from 2026-02-06 to 2026-02-07

## 1. 2026-02-06 16:01 — bryan@petoskeysoccer.com

Good Afternoon,

Below is a link to the Click Rd. Schedule for the Spring. This is the same
schedule that all premier teams have access to in order to avoid overlaps.
The major date to be aware of when scheduling is Saturday 5/9, PHS is
hosting a tournament at Click Rd.  The last date to schedule a premier game
is June 12th, please note that we are planning a second tournament for Rec
Teams (smaller in scale than the main event) at Click Rd. 6/6-6/7. We
should be able to accommodate a premier home game that weekend if necessary.

2026 MSPSP Click Rd Schedule/Conflicts
<https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1frrYuTa2ZAi_bFbLMeBxG_bSvuRBrKY1WX7u3fqO1ME/edit?usp=sharing>

*CLICK RD. NOTES*

   - Do not plan to have access to Click Rd. for games until April 18/19th
   - If you schedule a home game on 5/9 it needs to be after 2pm as the PHS
   girls are hosting a play date at Click Rd., no fields or refs before 2pm
   that day.
   - It’s extremely beneficial to schedule back to back games (not same
   team) to entice refs to travel.
   - 11am, 1pm, 3pm, and 5pm are game times to stick by if we need to
      adjust please note it on the shared schedule.
      - In the fall we had 3 games scheduled all at 1pm on the same day,
      please try to avoid this as it makes getting refs a major challenge.
   - I strongly discourage more than one home game per day for our Premier
   teams
   - It appears most GR schools have spring break the first full week in
   April.

*BLOCKED WEEKENDS*

   - Avoid scheduling games these weekends
      - May 8-10th, May 15-17th BASEBALL WEEKENDS
      - May 29-31st, Petoskey Tournament, Fresh Coast Classic

*MSPSP Notes*
It looks like we will have 4/5 home games and 4/5 away games. Overall,
travel looks manageable and west side centric.  Most likely we will take
two trips south and hopefully Gaylord is one of our away games.

Scheduling is set to go live this weekend.

9 league games, Petoskey Tournament (3/4 Games).
