# Petoskey Youth Soccer Association | Register to Secure Stephen's
 Roster Spot

**1 message** on 2025-06-09

## 1. 2025-06-09 18:00 — bryan@petoskeysoccer.com

Congratulations! Stephen Younker has been selected to be on our Petoskey Lakers 2012 Premier Team for Fall 2025. This team will play in the Michigan State Premier Soccer League, also competing in our home Petoskey Autumn Blast Tournament September 12-14, 2025.
Our team practices will begin in early August, where you will be competing for a starting position and playing time for our season. Games will start near the end of August, and our season typically ends in early November.
We will train two to three times a week—the dates and times will be determined when we review the players' schedules. Game scheduling starts in mid- to end-July.
You must accept and reserve or decline your position by June 12th at noon unless you have reached out to our club director, Bryan Goyings for an approved extension.
REGISTRATION
To register and confirm your roster position, please click here to log in to your account dashboard at PetoskeySoccer.com.
FEES
There are two options: pay in full today or make a deposit payment in the installment plan.
REQUIREMENTS
Your attention is required for a few action items;

Communication: We will use our web system's SpocketSports messaging app. The dashboard has instructions for downloading the app.
Uniform: We will follow up this week with uniform plans and links.
Required Paperwork and passcard photo: MSPSP requires that you sign your Player Contract and upload proof of age. Please complete this additional documentation at  https://www.sportsforms.club/mspsp.html. It will be collected via our website during your acceptance process. Please have a passport-style photo and proof of age documentation available.

QUESTIONS
If you have any team formation, registration, or payment questions, please contact Bryan Goyings at bryan@petoskeysoccer.com.
Thank you, we look forward to a great season!
